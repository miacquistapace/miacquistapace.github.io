Tetra Web Solutions helps create professional looking websites for individuals/small buisnesses. With the widespread use of the internet, more and more people require websites. We can create a variety of websites; from showcasing an individual's resume to buisnesses looking to advertise themselves over the internet, we can help. We prefer quality over quantity. We don't create websites for large scale corporations with complex backends, but rather for the college graduate that wants to make themselves stand out by having their own website, or the mom who's wanted to start a blog but wanted to make it personal. We take each and every project personaly, as if we were designing the website for us.

Tetra Web Solutions distinguishes itself by making a connection with the customer. We aren't just looking for your buisness, we want to help you make your dream website a reality. With that in mind, we don't abandon a customer after we finished their website. We understand that what you envisioned one day could change the next. We want to build long lasting relationships with our customers, and a great way we do that is by teaching our customers about how their website works. We program in a way that anyone can easily edit text/images on their website, with little to no prior knowledge. By putting comments detailing how certain parts of their website function, customers can implement their own changes. Let's say you'd want design changes and can't figure it out yourself. We provide quality customer support for 6 months, completley free of charge.
