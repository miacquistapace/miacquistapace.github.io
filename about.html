Biography

My name is Michael Acquistapace, a current junior at Redwood High School. Born in Russia but living in the Bay Area since I was 4, I've grown to love the technological culture surrounding the Bay Area. Web development has always been something that's interested me and thanks to Holberton School I was able to learn how to design this very website in a matter of just three weeks.

Redwood High School

Redwood High School first exposed me to computer programming as a freshman with it's Computer Programming 1-2 class. This class taught me the fundamentals of programming. As a sophmore I took AP Computer Science which further expanded my knowledge of Object Oriented Programming and taught me various useful skills which can apply to multiple languages (recursion, arrays, etc). Outside of regular classes, Redwood offers students oppurtunities to prepare for college aswell as providing support groups for all students. As one of the best pubic schools in the SF Bay Area, I am glad that I live in an area where this school is avaliable.

Why I built this website

After learning the basics of webdevelopment, I wanted to apply what I learned into something productive. This website is the culmination of three weeks of learning HTML/CSS/JS. I've had the idea to do freelance web design for about a year now, and thanks to my newfound knowledge I can make that idea a reality. I believe I can bring up my skills and make my website look more professional in the future.
